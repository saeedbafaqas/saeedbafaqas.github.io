Welcome to VirtoCloud

A good design starts with a good code

Branch Connectivity

Virtocloud Branch connectivity allows you to connect your branches to your head office through internet without the need for expensive MPLS/Leased Lines services on top of that our services activation is instant you don’t to wait for months to get your branch up and running.

Smart Remote-Access and Things

Virtocloud Smart Remote Access solution provide you the flexibility of connecting your teams, suppliers, partners and machines such point of sales and IoT to your network and assign them to the right privilege levels they need to do their work as they don't need full access to your network they only can access they services or applications they need.

Backup Links

You already have your branches connected through MPLS and Leased lines but they are not redundant and its highly expensive to have redundant MPLS links and the hassle of having different service providers Virtocloud hybrid WAN solution allows you to have secondary links through internet and connect your branch securely and ensure the highest availability from having dual connectivity using different technologies.

MPLS Alternative

Services such MPLS and Leased lines are outdated, limited speed, highly expensive and its get even worst for international connected branches, with virtocloud networking platform saves you over 80% and grantee higher speeds and better performance plus high security standards.

Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity

Disasters may strike anytime and leave bad impact on business, having disaster recovery is so important, with virtocloud wither your disaster recovery site on premises or on the cloud we can connect it with your branches and route your traffic to the active data center and it can be done automatically though our disaster recovery and network monitoring solution.
